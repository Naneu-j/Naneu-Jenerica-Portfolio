About Me

I am a Full-Stack Software Developer with a strong foundation in Computer Science and a passion for building elegant, high-performance digital systems. My work combines clean user experience design with scalable backend architecture, ensuring that every solution is both refined and technically robust.

With core programming expertise in Python, C, and C++, I approach development with a systems-oriented mindset. I specialize in modern web technologies including HTML, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS for frontend development, and Node.js, Express, Django, and Flask for backend systems. I work confidently with databases such as MongoDB and MySQL.

My current focus is on AI integration within FinTech, EdTech, and Health Systems, alongside strengthening my expertise in Cybersecurity — particularly secure backend design and application security. I am deeply interested in building scalable architectures that power impactful digital platforms.

Long-term, my vision is to contribute to Africa’s technological growth by developing resilient, intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. I am also building Lenasola, a modern technology solutions company focused on delivering scalable and innovative digital services.
